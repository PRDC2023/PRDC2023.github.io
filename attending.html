General Information

Due to the strict border control in China dealing with the COVID-19 pandemic, we have unfortunately decided to run the conference virtually. PRDC 2023 will run virtually using Microsoft Teams. All registered attendees will receive an email containing the attendance detail one week before the conference start date.

Registration Policies

Authors are expected to present accepted papers at the conference.
At least one author per paper must be registered as an AUTHOR at the full registration rate (at either IEEE member or non-member rate), regardless of whether or not they are students; further, a registration of any additional paper requires a full author registration fee.

Registration Modifications

Any modification to the registration should be addressed to "registration+PRDC@computer.org" with the subject "[PRDC22] Request for Modification of the Registration". See the Refund Policy below for more details on modifications concerning the rates and/or registration items.

Refund Policy

All refund/cancellation requests must be received in writing to "registration+PRDC@computer.org" by 22 November, 2023, 11:59 PM Eastern Time. There will be an administrative fee of US$120 (US$10) for cancelled Author (Non-Author) registrations from each cancellation. You may only cancel your registration before the aforementioned cancellation deadline.
